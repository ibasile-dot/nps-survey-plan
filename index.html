1. What we're solving

No structured, recurring feedback loop from organisers. No NPS baseline. No comparable data across clients or over time. Post-event surveys historically fail (low response). We need measurable data that feeds into product decisions, retention strategy, and OKR tracking.

2. Model: Event-cycle micro-surveys

4 short surveys triggered at different points in the event lifecycle. Each takes ~3 minutes. Questions are relevant to what the organiser is actually doing at that stage.

#SurveyWhenSent byTypeform
S1Onboarding & Setup2-4 weeks after kickoffRMOpen S1 survey
S2Pre-Event2-3 weeks before event goes liveRMOpen S2 survey
S3Post-Event1-2 weeks after event endsRMOpen S3 survey
S4Renewal / Annual~1 month before contract renewalRMOpen S4 survey

RM shares the link after the relevant call. If not completed in 7 days, walks through it together on the next call (screen share).

Why RM-led: Clients are more likely to share honest feedback with RMs than TAMs (TAMs are too close to daily work). Historical post-event surveys sent by email consistently failed. RMs share the link first; if not completed, they walk through it together on the next call.

Delivery flow

  1. PlanHat task triggers — Based on event date, PlanHat creates a task for the RM: "Send [Survey Name] to [Client]"
  2. RM sends Typeform link — Via email after the relevant call (or during the call if timing is right)
  3. 7-day window — If organiser hasn't completed the survey within 7 days, PlanHat reminds the RM
  4. Fallback: on-call — RM walks through the survey on the next strategy call (screen share)
  5. Data flows to Typeform dashboard — Scores, trends, open-text responses all visible in Typeform analytics + Google Sheets export

Coverage expectations

  • Most clients hit 2-3 surveys per year (not all 4)
  • S3 (Post-Event) is the priority — this is where NPS lives
  • Target: 50% response rate from surveyed organisers in first quarter
  • Multi-event contracts: one survey per event (not per contract)

RM Strategy Call Framework

Optional prompt cards for monthly strategy calls. Pick 2-3 questions based on where the client is in their event cycle. These mirror the survey themes so you get qualitative context alongside the scores.

What's missing today

  • Data from calls isn't comparable across clients — different topics every time
  • No call-to-call continuity — actions and follow-ups get lost
  • Fathom records everything but there's no structured capture of insights

Prompt cards by event stage

These are different from the survey questions. The survey captures scores. These go deeper — they're conversational follow-ups that give context behind the numbers.

StageSurvey asks (scores)Call goes deeper (conversation)Tracks
Post-KickoffHow easy was it to get started? (1-10)
  • Walk me through how you approached the setup — what did you tackle first?
  • When you got stuck, what did you do? Who did you go to?
  • What did you expect the platform to do that it didn't?
Onboarding friction points, support dependency patterns, expectation gaps
Pre-EventHow satisfied are you so far? (1-10)
  • What's your biggest worry about the event going live?
  • Tell me about the last time you had to contact support — what happened?
  • If you had an extra hour this week, what would you spend it on?
Readiness risks, support triggers, time-consuming tasks
Post-EventWould you recommend us? (NPS 0-10)
  • What moment during the event made you think "this works" or "this doesn't"?
  • What feedback did you get from your own attendees or exhibitors?
  • If you ran this event again tomorrow, what would you change on our side?
Platform performance patterns, end-user feedback, improvement priorities
Pre-RenewalHas the platform gotten better/same/worse?
  • What's the conversation internally about the platform — what do your colleagues say?
  • What's the one thing that would make this decision easy for you?
  • Is there a tool or workflow outside our platform that you wish was inside it?
Internal perception, retention levers, competitive gaps
