NPS & Organiser Feedback — Full Plan

Owner: Ilaria Basile  |  Updated: 10 April 2026  |  Epic: EP-50795  |  Google Sheet Dashboard  |  Status: In Progress

1. What we're solving

No structured feedback loop from organisers. No NPS baseline. No comparable data across clients. We need measurable data that feeds product decisions, retention strategy, and OKR tracking — and a monthly understanding of progress, not just a yearly one.

2. Three surveys

Based on feedback from Barry and Oriana (April 10): merged the onboarding and pre-event surveys (for first-time clients these overlap), and reframed the renewal survey as an annual review. Each survey: 5 questions, ~3 minutes. All built in Typeform, auto-connected to Google Sheets.

#SurveyWhenPurposeTypeform
S1Setup & Configuration2-4 weeks after kickoffCaptures onboarding experience, self-service level, platform friction areas. Critical for new clients — shows we're proactive and listening.Open survey
S2Post-EventBetween event end and debrief callCore survey. NPS, overall satisfaction, product and service feedback. Results feed into the debrief call and give us a comparable baseline across all clients.Open survey
S3Annual ReviewOnce per year — RM decides timingStrategic view: platform trajectory, investment priorities, competitive gaps. Understanding the overall relationship — not a renewal conversation.Open survey

3. How it works

  1. PlanHat task triggers — Based on event date, PlanHat creates a task for the RM. RMs can adjust dates or postpone with a reason.
  2. RM sends Typeform link 7 days before the relevant call — Gives the organiser time to complete it independently.
  3. If not completed by the call — RM walks through the survey on screen in the first 5 minutes.
  4. Data flows automatically — Typeform → Google Sheet Dashboard (NPS, CSAT, friction areas all auto-calculated).

Why RM-led: Clients are more honest with RMs than TAMs. Historical email surveys consistently failed. This approach uses the existing relationship and call schedule.

RM Strategy Call Playbook

A best-practice resource for strategy calls — not a script. Conversation prompts to draw from when the moment is right. These are different from the survey questions: the survey captures scores, these go deeper.

Why this matters: Having structured, comparable feedback across all clients gives us a monthly understanding of progress — not just a yearly one. This is critical for OKR 3 (CSAT target) and for Tanya's visibility into how the platform is tracking. Every data point counts. The more consistent the feedback, the faster we can act on it.
StageConversation prompts (pick 2-3)
Setup / Onboarding
  • Walk me through how you approached the setup — what did you tackle first?
  • When you got stuck, what did you do? Who did you go to?
  • What did you expect the platform to do that it didn't?
Pre-Event
  • What's your biggest worry about the event going live?
  • Tell me about the last time you had to contact support — what happened?
  • If you had an extra hour this week, what would you spend it on?
Post-Event
  • What moment during the event made you think "this works" or "this doesn't"?
  • What feedback did you get from your own attendees or exhibitors?
  • If you ran this event again tomorrow, what would you change on our side?
Annual / Relationship
  • What's the conversation internally about the platform — what do your colleagues say?
  • What's the one thing that would make this decision easy for you?
  • Is there a tool or workflow outside our platform that you wish was inside it?

Phase 2: May — PlanHat integration

Once PlanHat is set up, survey data connects to client records for a full picture.

  • Survey scores appear on the client record in PlanHat alongside usage data and support tickets
  • PlanHat triggers survey tasks based on event dates — RMs see "Send Post-Event survey to [Client]" and can adjust or postpone
  • RM call logs (sentiment, top issue, key insight) create call-to-call continuity
  • Client health score combines survey data + call sentiment + usage + support tickets
  • NPS and CSAT metrics feed into the OKR dashboard

Connect survey feedback with actual platform usage to understand the full picture.

  • Link survey responses with Mixpanel/Posthog usage data — if a client rates setup as difficult, what are they actually doing in the admin panel? Where do they drop off?
